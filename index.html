Sports For Kids

Bringing sports to the kids of the Bryan-College Station Area

Events

For the Kids

Show & Tell

Giving kids the opportunity to go to two A&M sporting events each semester. Bring your kid to the drop off location and let us take care of the rest!

Sports Fest

A special event at the end of each semester where A&M athletes come out to play along with the kids. Kids get a unique opportunity to play different sports with the top players of each sport!

Sports Saturday

Fun-filled Saturdays for the kids to get active and play a variety of games and sports. Tug-of-war and 4 square are just a couple of our favorites!

Events

Member Focused

Socials

Events throughout the semester to hangout with other members of the org. Past socials are pumpkin carving, s'mores night, Lake Bryan social, and so many more!

Intramurals

Name an intramural sport and we have a team for it! No org compares to SFK in our number of sports nor our strong record of wins!

Fundraising

Funding the org with lots of merch options and profit shares. You get to eat chipotle and SFK gets a portion of the cost! Eat a burrito for the kids!

Stay updated on all our upcoming events to ensure your kid never misses the fun. No spam, just event dates, locations, and signup details.
