Frequently Asked Questions

01

Sports For Kids is a student-led organization at Texas A&M University. We all love sports and we want to bring that joy to the kids of the Bryan/College Station area.

02

All activities are free for families! Events are funded through the organization's finance committee.

03

We take safety very seriously and so all student members have passed yearly background checks. Additionally, through the Texas A&M Uviversity system, all members complete child protection training (CPT) every two years.

04

Join our email list through the form above and we will keep you up to date with upcoming events. For specific events, emails will contain a sign up form for your child or children.
